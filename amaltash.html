Basic Information

  • Common Name: Amaltash
  • English Name: Golden Shower Tree
  • Scientific Name: Cassia fistula
  • Family: Fabaceae
  • Native To: Indian Subcontinent

Features of Amaltash Tree

  • Famous for long hanging clusters of bright yellow flowers
  • Medium-sized deciduous tree
  • Blooms mainly during summer
  • Highly ornamental and attractive

Uses & Benefits

  • Used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine
  • Helps improve air quality
  • Provides shade and beauty to surroundings
  • Attracts birds and pollinators

Interesting Facts

  • National flower of Thailand
  • Symbol of prosperity and happiness
  • One of the most beautiful flowering trees in India
