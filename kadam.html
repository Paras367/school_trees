Basic Information

  • Common Name: Kadam
  • English Name: Burflower Tree
  • Scientific Name: Neolamarckia cadamba
  • Family: Rubiaceae
  • Native To: South and Southeast Asia

Features of Kadam Tree

  • Large deciduous tree with a broad canopy
  • Produces round, fragrant orange flowers
  • Fast-growing and long-living tree
  • Provides dense shade

Uses & Importance

  • Wood used in furniture and paper industries
  • Flowers are important for pollinators
  • Used in traditional medicine
  • Planted along roads and parks
