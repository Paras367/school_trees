Basic Information

  • Common Name: Bamboo
  • Scientific Name: Bambusoideae
  • Family: Poaceae (Grass family)
  • Native To: Asia, Africa, and the Americas
üå± Bamboo is one of the fastest-growing plants on Earth.

Features of Bamboo

  • Evergreen perennial plant
  • Hollow stems called culms
  • Can grow up to 91 cm in a single day
  • Very strong yet flexible

Uses & Benefits

  • Used in construction, furniture, and handicrafts
  • Edible bamboo shoots are nutritious
  • Used in paper, textile, and biofuel industries
  • Acts as a natural windbreak

Environmental Importance

  • Absorbs more carbon dioxide than most trees
  • Releases high amounts of oxygen
  • Prevents soil erosion
  • Supports wildlife and biodiversity

Interesting Facts

  • Bamboo is technically a grass, not a tree
  • It is 100% biodegradable
  • Symbol of strength and resilience
