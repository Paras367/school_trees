Project Overview

The School Trees QR Code Project is an eco-friendly and educational initiative aimed at spreading awareness about trees present inside the school campus.

Each tree is tagged with a QR code.
When scanned, the QR code opens a dedicated web page that displays:

  • Tree name (Common & Local)
  • Scientific name (Botanical classification)
  • Features, medicinal & ecological benefits
  • Environmental importance & conservation status
  • Eco-awareness messages for students

This initiative combines technology with environmental responsibility to foster sustainable learning.

Project Impact

50+ Trees Cataloged
100% QR Coverage
200+ Students Engaged
12 Species Protected

Key Objectives

  • Promote awareness about local biodiversity and ecosystem balance
  • Encourage students to observe, document, and learn about campus flora
  • Integrate digital tools (QR codes, web tech) for experiential environmental education
  • Support NSS motto: NOT ME BUT YOU through community action
  • Develop student leadership in sustainability initiatives
