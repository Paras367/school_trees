Features of Ficus Tree

  • Evergreen or semi-evergreen in nature
  • Strong trunk with spreading canopy
  • Known for aerial roots in some species
  • Highly adaptable to different environments

Environmental Importance

  • Improves air quality by absorbing pollutants
  • Provides shade and cooling effect
  • Supports birds, insects, and small animals
  • Helps in soil binding and erosion control

Cultural & Practical Uses

  • Widely used as an ornamental and avenue tree
  • Many species are sacred in Indian culture
  • Common indoor plant in urban areas

Interesting Facts

  • Some Ficus trees can live for hundreds of years
  • Banyan tree is one of the largest Ficus species
  • Ficus plants help reduce indoor stress levels
