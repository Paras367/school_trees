NSS Tree QR Awareness Project

Purpose of This Website

This website is created purely for educational, environmental, and awareness purposes under the National Service Scheme (NSS).

What Users CAN Do

  • Scan QR codes placed on school trees for educational access
  • Read and learn about trees, biodiversity, and environmental conservation
  • Use the information for academic projects, research, and classroom teaching
  • Share knowledge responsibly with proper attribution to NSS & SSIS
  • Provide constructive feedback to improve the project

What Users CANNOT Do

  • Modify, alter, misuse, or misrepresent the content without permission
  • Use this project for commercial purposes, monetization, or profit
  • Attempt hacking, spamming, scraping, or unauthorized data extraction
  • Use content in a misleading, defamatory, or harmful manner
  • Remove or obscure copyright notices, credits, or attribution

Privacy & Safety

This website does NOT collect personal data. No login, cookies, analytics, or tracking systems are used.

  • Zero data collection policy
  • All interactions remain local to your browser
  • Secure HTTPS hosting via GitHub Pages
  • Mobile-optimized, no app installation required
