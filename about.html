What is NSS?

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a Government of India initiative aimed at developing social responsibility, leadership, and community service among students.

NSS Motto: "Not Me But You" — encouraging selfless service and dedication towards society and the nation.

Purpose of the Project

This project is part of the NSS awareness activities under the themes "Colours of Indian Culture" and "Swachh Bharat Abhiyan".

  • Educate students and visitors about trees on the school campus
  • Promote environmental responsibility and eco-consciousness
  • Encourage cleanliness, sustainability, and green practices
  • Use technology (QR codes) for interactive, modern learning

How It Works

1 Scan QR Code Use your phone camera on any campus tree tag
2 Open Web Page Instant access to tree information
3 Learn & Explore Discover scientific names, benefits & more
4 Share Knowledge Spread awareness with friends & family

Project Journey

Concept Phase
NSS volunteers brainstormed eco-education ideas aligned with Swachh Bharat
Tree Survey
Cataloged 16+ tree species across Saint Soldiers International School campus
Development
Created static web pages with QR integration using GitHub Pages
Deployment
Printed weather-resistant QR tags and installed on campus trees
Education Launch
Students and visitors now explore nature through technology

About Saint Soldiers International School

Saint Soldiers International School, Sector 28-B, Chandigarh, is a CBSE-affiliated, English-medium, co-educational institution known for academic excellence, discipline, and holistic development.

The school emphasizes values, cultural awareness, sports, technology, and social responsibility, preparing students to become confident and responsible citizens.

For Visitors & New Teachers

Welcome to our campus! Each tree is tagged with a QR code. When scanned, it opens a dedicated webpage providing:

  • Tree name (English, Scientific & Hindi)
  • Features, medicinal properties & ecological benefits
  • Environmental importance & conservation status
  • Eco-awareness messages for sustainable living

We invite you to explore, learn, and support environmental conservation.
