Features of Mahua Tree

  • Medium to large-sized deciduous tree
  • Thick trunk with spreading branches
  • Produces small, fleshy, cream-colored flowers
  • Drought-resistant and long-living tree

Uses & Importance

  • Flowers used in traditional food and drinks
  • Seeds used to extract Mahua oil
  • Oil used in cooking, soaps, and medicines
  • Important livelihood source for tribal communities

Environmental & Cultural Importance

  • Supports biodiversity and soil conservation
  • Provides shade and shelter to animals
  • Holds religious and cultural significance
  • Helps in sustainable rural economy
